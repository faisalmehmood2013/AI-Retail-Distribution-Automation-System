## 2. 🚀 Key Features

---

### **1. End-to-End Smart Customer Ordering & Invoicing System**

#### 🛒 Overview

A fully automated ordering flow that allows customers to place orders through WhatsApp or any chat platform without any manual effort.

#### ⭐ Benefits

* Faster customer response time
* Zero manual data entry
* Fully automated invoice generation
* Accurate and real-time stock updates
* Improves customer satisfaction with instant service

#### 🔧 Customer Workflow

###### **1. Product Inquiry**

* Customer sends message on WhatsApp/chat
* System provides complete product information:

  * Name
  * Price
  * Category
  * Size/Variant
  * Stock availability

###### **2. Order Confirmation**

* Customer selects product + quantity
* System captures the order automatically
* Order saved to **Customer Order Sheet**

###### **3. Invoice Process**

* Order automatically copied to **Invoice Sheet**
* Automatically invoice generated instantly
* Invoice emailed to customer

###### **4. Backend Automation**

* Inventory updated in real-time
* Sales register updated automatically
* Customer support through FAQs & company info

---

### **2. End-to-End Automated Purchase, Supplier & Low Stock & Expiry Email Alerts**

#### 🏭 Overview

A powerful automated system that handles your entire purchase cycle, supplier communication, and inventory restocking without manual work.

#### ⭐ Benefits

* No need to manually contact suppliers
* Automatic purchase order notifications
* Real-time stock control
* Prevents both overstock and expiry losses
* Ensures business continuity with low-stock alerts

#### 🔧 Purchase Workflow

###### **1. Purchase Order Creation**

* Purchase generated via WhatsApp/chat
* Order automatically saved in **Purchase Sheet**
* Supplier/company receives automatic email

###### **2. Stock Receiving Process**

* Supplier sends goods
* User confirms receiving through chat
* System updates **Received Stock** automatically
* Inventory adjusted instantly

###### **3. Backend Automation**

* Purchase register updated without manual work
* Helps maintain accurate purchase history

###### **4. Smart Alerts System**

* **Low-stock alert** sent via email
* **Near-expiry alert** sent automatically
* Prevents shortages & expiry losses
